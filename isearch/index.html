Wanted Poster

Poem

a poem of lament, of the streaming service

When I sat down one night
for some nice relaxation I made a selection of some fine entertainment
So a screen faded in, and it had many colors
But it was, to my horor,
AN AD.

I paid for this device.
I paid for this service.
So tell me, then, why is there
AN AD.
